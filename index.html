Yunji Kim

I am a research scientist at NAVER AI Lab, where I've worked on generative models and representation learning. I completed my Master's degree in Computer Science at Yonsei University under the supervision of Prof. Seon Joo Kim. Previously, I received my B.S. in Astronomy and Physics at Yonsei University.

Recently, my focus has been on detecting fake images generated by diffusion models and understanding how large models infer their outputs.
